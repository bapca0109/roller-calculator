<analysis>**original_problem_statement**: The user wants to create a mobile application to calculate the price of belt conveyor rollers, serving as an engineering and quoting tool. The scope has expanded to include a product catalog search, a full admin panel for price management, a customer database, and a complete quote/RFQ generation and approval workflow with file attachments and product-specific remarks.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS**:
- **Core Function**: Calculate prices for Carrying, Impact, and Return belt conveyor rollers.
- **Admin Panel**: Secure interface for CRUD operations, customer management, and sales analytics.
- **Customer Management**: System to manage customers, with filters for 'Registered' vs. 'Quoted'. Admins can view all quotes for a specific customer.
- **Professional UI**: A corporate look and feel with a Carmine Red color scheme.
- **Authentication**: Secure OTP-based signup, Forgot Password functionality, and auto-logout after 7 days of inactivity.
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)**: Distinct UIs for Customers and Admins. Prices are hidden from customers until a quote is formally approved.
- **RFQ & Quote Workflow**:
    - A full lifecycle from customer RFQ submission (with remarks/attachments) to admin review and approval.
    - Approved RFQs become official Quotes, with the quote date reflecting the approval time.
    - Quotes are traceable back to the original RFQ number.
- **Admin Dashboard & Analytics**: A comprehensive dashboard showing KPIs (revenue, quotes, customers), charts, and top customer lists, with date-range filters and export to PDF/Excel.
- **Search & Export**: Search functionality across quotes, customers, and the product catalog, with the ability to export search results.
- **Professional PDF Quotes**: A redesigned, minimalist quote PDF that includes detailed commercial and technical terms and conditions.

**User's preferred language**: English
