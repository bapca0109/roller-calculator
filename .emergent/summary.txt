**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS**:
- **Core Function**: Calculate prices for Carrying, Impact, and Return belt conveyor rollers.

- **Customer Management**: System to manage customers with unique, auto-incrementing customer codes. A designation field was recently added to the signup process.
- **Professional UI**: A corporate, minimalist, and aesthetic look and feel with a Carmine Red color scheme.
- **Authentication**: Secure OTP-based signup and login.
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)**: Prices are hidden from customers until a quote is formally approved.
- **RFQ & Quote Workflow**: A full lifecycle from customer RFQ submission to admin review, edit, approval, or rejection. Admins must now also create RFQs first.
- **Data Export**: Ability to export quotes, customer lists, and search results to PDF and Excel.
- **PDF Customization**: PDFs must include the company logo (Convero), a generation timestamp, and detailed weight information. The discount column has been removed in favor of a Value after discount column.
- **Quote Revision History**: Admins and Customers need to see a history of all changes made to a quote after it's approved.
- **Push Notifications**: Admins should receive a push notification on their mobile app whenever a new RFQ is submitted by a customer.
- **Live Freight Calculation**: Freight must be calculated dynamically based on total weight and distance. Admins can override this with a custom amount.
- **Company Branding**: All generated documents (PDFs, emails) must contain the full, standardized company address and branding.
